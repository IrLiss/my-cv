About me

Iryna Shapavalava

29 years old

My path as a designer and programmer began in my school years. I used to spend hours sketching websites and writing their markup using a modest knowledge of Photoshop, HTML, CSS. At that time, I just did not know the exciting challenges that were yet to come.

Now I work as a freelance illustrator, sometimes I design and markup websites. For over three years now, I'm having the chance to work with marvelous people, learning every day, traveling the world, and mainly, always making great friendships.

It was the work of a freelance illustrator that taught me to adapt to different customers and implement different creative ideas. I have improved skills to work with graphic tablet, became an advanced user of Photoshop and Illustrator. Working remotely gives me more free time to spend learning front-end development. I believe that excellent communication skills and an inquisitive mind will have help me become an experienced front-end developer.

Nowadays, I keep seeking new skills, learning and sharing my humble knowledge as much as possible.
